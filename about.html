About Excel Vehicle Transportation

Reliable, professional vehicle collection and delivery services exclusively for the motor trade.

Excel Vehicle Transportation provides reliable, professional vehicle collection and delivery services exclusively for the motor trade.

We work with dealerships, dealer groups, fleet and leasing operators, auctions, and bodyshops — businesses who need vehicles moved efficiently, safely and on time.

Our service is built around clear communication, professional drivers, and dependable execution. We understand that every vehicle we move represents your business, your customer relationship, and your reputation. That's why we treat every movement as if our own business depends on it — because it does.

Whether it's a single dealer transfer, a multi-drop fleet movement, or an urgent auction collection, we provide a straightforward, no-nonsense service that the trade can rely on.

We don't overcomplicate things. You email the details, we confirm availability, a professional driver is assigned, and the job gets done. Updates throughout, confirmation on delivery.

Based in Yorkshire and operating daily across the UK, we're building a reputation for doing what we say we'll do — every time.
